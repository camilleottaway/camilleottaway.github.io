Projects

USGS Wave Visualization

Worked with a team to create a website with interactive data visualization software that provides wave forecasts for up to 48 hours as well as current wave observation data. This uses model and real time observations of windspeed, wave height, tidal information, and geographic features to predict water conditions.

Nimble Know

Lead a team to create a prototype free, accessible, instant feedback system. Integrated text-to-speech and translation makes it easy for students to participate and teachers to give feedback. Designed system architecture, user interface, and database. Performed one round of user testing.

POND: A Social Media Platform for Parents of NueroDiverse

Worked with a team to create a social media network specifically for parents and family of children on the autistic spectrum with the goal of providing a secure and private platform for the neurodiverse community to connect and share resources. Our proof of concept project included a tagging and searching system so quality content could remain accessible.

Towards Understanding the Effective Design of Automated Formative Feedback for Programming Assignments (2020)

Studied the effects of the design of automated formative feedback on student learning. Analyzed student performance data. Found feedback addressing the gap between actual and expected outcome is critical to learning. Published as co-author in National Computer Science Education
