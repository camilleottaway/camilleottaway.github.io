SEIR Model of Covid-19 Social Relaxation Measures

Collaborated to research effects of different social distancing strategies on the number of people infected with Covid-19 at one time. Used Susceptible Exposed Infectious Recovered (SEIR) Model implemented in Python. Found that social distancing measures greatly reduced the number of infected people at once compared to no social distancing measures. Determined that at least a month of isolation is important to keep infections below threshold values.

Python, Javascript, HTML
